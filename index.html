Web
Designer & developer

As a skilled web developer and problem solver, I craft bespoke websites and digital solutions that streamline business operations and enhance online presence. With a keen eye for design and a deep understanding of technology, I deliver tailored solutions that not only look great, but also function seamlessly to meet the unique needs of each individual client.

My favorite tools for design and prototyping are Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, AfterEffects and more. To create solutions I use PowerAutomate, PowerApp and SharePoint, and to develope websites I use technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript and ReactJS.
